Q: difference between Factory & abstract Factory Pattern. Disadvantages of Factory pattern over abstract factory?
Answer:
Factory pattern is used to create a single product object, while Abstract Factory is used to create a family of related objects without specifying their concrete classes. The disadvantage of Factory pattern over Abstract Factory is that it is less suitable when we need consistency across multiple related objects, and it becomes harder to scale for families of products.”
Very short answer

Factory creates one object.
Abstract Factory creates a group of related objects.
Factory is simpler, but less suitable for product families.
