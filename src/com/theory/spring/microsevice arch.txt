“At the top, users connect through a CDN and API Gateway for routing, caching, and security.
Inside Kubernetes, we deploy independent domain microservices like Auth, Product, Order, Payment, and Inventory — each with its own database.
They communicate asynchronously through Kafka and synchronously via REST/gRPC.
Cross-cutting concerns such as metrics, logging, tracing, and security are managed through a service mesh and observability stack.
Finally, everything is deployed via GitOps CI/CD pipelines with autoscaling and high availability.”
